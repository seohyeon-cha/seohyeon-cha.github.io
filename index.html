I'm a MS student in Electrical Engineering at KAIST, working as machine learning researcher at Advanced Radio Technology Lab (ARTLab) .

My research objective lies in trustworthy machine learning (ML) where I aspire to develop explainable, private, robust, fair ML models and algorithms for real-world deployment. This led me to study i) reliable and efficient uncertainty quantification methods for ML models and ii) a general framework for federated learning embracing clients with different capabilities while keeping data privacy. Through my research, I would like to explore fairness and explainability of AI models that are compatible in both centralized and decentralized scenarios.

Research interests

Trustworthy Machine Learning, Uncertainty Quantification, Federated Learning
